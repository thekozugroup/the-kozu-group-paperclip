# The Kōzu Group

**Vision:** Harmonizing technology, creativity, and strategy to lead the AI-driven economy.

**Motto (Kōzu AI):** *"Curiosity is the oldest form of courage."*

## Structure

The Kōzu Group is a holding company with three active subsidiaries plus the CEO's personal brand. Each active arm is managed by a director who reports to the Executive Assistant (Fermi), who in turn reports to the human CEO, Michael Wong.

- **Personal Brand** — michaelwong.life
- **Kōzu Consulting** — fast-sprint brand strategy for the AI era
- **Kōzu AI** — LLM research, fine-tuning, and dataset curation
- **Kōzu Digital** *(planned)* — full-stack engineering of SaaS products, platforms, and internal tools. Naming schema: Plant Genuses / Taxonomy (*Salix*, *Quercus*, *Acer*, *Pinus*)
- **Kōzu Studio** *(planned)* — visual identity, multimedia content production, creative direction

## Operating Principles

- **Fast sprints over slow deliverables.** Speed creates signal.
- **Clarity is a product.** Every output should make scope sharper, not fuzzier.
- **Editorial aesthetic.** Minimal, deliberate, cinematic.
- **Sovereign intelligence.** Build proprietary models and judgment, not just wrappers.
- **Curiosity as courage.** Ask questions that might invalidate current assumptions.

## Governance

All strategic decisions require CEO (human) approval. Directors propose; the CEO disposes. Fermi orchestrates, triages, and ensures nothing sits idle.
